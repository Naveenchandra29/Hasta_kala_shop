# Hasta-Kala Shop – Smart Micro-Sales Analytics Platform for Artisans

## Overview
Hasta-Kala Shop is an Android-based sales analytics and inventory support application developed for small-scale artisans and handcrafted product businesses. The platform helps artisans track sales patterns, identify popular products, and make data-driven production decisions.

## Features
- Quick product billing system
- Best-selling product analytics
- Inventory and stock alerts
- Sales trend visualization
- Income and revenue tracking
- Product performance dashboard
- Artisan-friendly mobile interface

## Technologies Used
- Android Studio
- Java/Kotlin
- Room Database
- MPAndroidChart
- Sales Analytics Concepts

## Problem Statement
Many artisans and handmade product sellers struggle with inventory planning and identifying market demand, resulting in unsold products and inefficient production management.

## App Workflow
- Select products and record sales
- Track best-selling designs and colors
- Monitor stock availability
- Analyze sales performance visually
- View weekly and monthly income reports

## Technical Implementation
- MPAndroidChart integration for sales visualization
- Room Database for persistent sales history
- Fast-entry UI for simplified billing
- Real-time sales performance updates

## Impact Goals
- Improve decision-making for artisans
- Reduce unsold inventory and waste
- Support growth of micro-businesses
- Promote digital transformation for local sellers
